About Doris

Hey, I'm Doris.

I spent eighteen years in finance, climbing through the PE and VC world, working alongside founders and leaders who looked like they had everything handled. I looked like that too. On the inside I was running on empty, holding it all together right up until the day I could not, and the burnout I had been outrunning finally caught me.

That was the moment everything changed. In 2019 I stopped performing my way through life and started doing the deeper work, with my body, my breath and the stories I had been telling myself for years. It gave me back something I had quietly given up on, a sense of calm and clarity that did not depend on the next win.

This matters to me because I know how lonely that high-functioning emptiness feels, and I know it does not have to be the price of success. I do this work so that the people still sitting where I sat do not have to lose themselves to get there.
